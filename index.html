Location

The heart of Santa Teresa

Santa Teresa sits on the southern tip of Costa Rica's Nicoya Peninsula — a Blue Zone destination known for natural beauty, incredible surf, and a thriving wellness culture. The town has a single main road lined with restaurants, yoga studios, surf shops, and cozy cafés, all backed by lush jungle and facing the Pacific Ocean.

Wet Sand is positioned right in the middle of it all. Walk to dinner, rent a surfboard, grab a fresh smoothie, or simply cross the road and head straight to the beach.
